Integrate Quartz Scheduler for triggered execution

The trigger mechanism is provided by Quartz Scheduler and allows to profit from the powerful Quartz scheduling capabilities.

This makes Scout Scheduler obsolete, so that concurrent and timed execution is all done by Scout JobManager.
