DESCRIPTION:

Show own page menus on detail form

If a page-with-nodes contains a detail form, the menus from the page are
copied to the detail form so that the action can directly be performed
in the form.
The menus to be copied must be the ones from the page on which the
detail form is registered (not the ones currently active on the
outline). Otherwise the menus point to wrong elements.
