WizardProgressField: add new design

A new design for the WizardProgressField is added. Each step will be
displayed with an icon (configured icon or number of the step). Steps
have a new 'finished' property. Given that 'finished' is set to true and
the step has no icon configured, the displayed step icon will change to
scout.icons.CHECKED_BOLD.
