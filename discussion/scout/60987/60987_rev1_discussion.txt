DESCRIPTION:

Add functionality to associate jobs with execution hints

This functionality is used by HTML UI, e.g. to stop waiting for jobs which require an user interaction.

Execution hints can be evaluated by filters like when listening to job lifecycle events, or when waiting for job completion, or by the job manager.
