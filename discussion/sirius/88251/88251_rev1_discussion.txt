DESCRIPTION:

[510085] EEF Specifier Properties: generates groups, pages and widgets 

On the specifier side, on the ViewDescription element, add the
possibility to automatically generates appropriate widgets (with all
required fields filled) for all structural features of all domain
classes of the selected metamodel package.
The generated widgets should be put under groups. These groups should be
linked with pages.
To enable this feature, the specifier should fill the metamodels field
from the "Properties" model element.
