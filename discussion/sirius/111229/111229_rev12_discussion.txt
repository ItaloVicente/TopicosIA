DESCRIPTION:

[527047] Capability to adapt size when exporting diagrams as image

Before this issue, there was only the capability to activate the
auto-scaling. With this option, generated image was bigger, had best
quality and was longer to generate. Now there is the capability to adapt
between nominal size (as with previous auto-scaling option disabled)
and maximum size of image (as with previous auto-scaling option
enabled).
