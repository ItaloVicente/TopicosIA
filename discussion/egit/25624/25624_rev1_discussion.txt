Interactive Rebase View: Action buttons toggle

If a rebase action button (e.g. "squash") is already pushed in, clicking
it again now switches the action for the selected rebase lines to
"pick". This allows toggling between "pick" and another action by
repeatedly clicking the button of the other action.
