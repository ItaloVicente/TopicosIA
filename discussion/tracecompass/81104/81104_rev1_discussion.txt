DESCRIPTION:

timing.ui: add In-N-Out analysis

This is set of views designed for the polarsys rover.

It is based on the notion of trace tuples entering and exiting
a scope, hence in, and out.

* In-N-Out table is the list of the tuples. Used for precise
    investigation
* In-N-Out statistics are used for an overview
* In-N-Out scatter is to view the data layout of scopes
* In-N-Out density view is to view the outlyers
* In-N-Out chart a chart modeling the rover, with the period and
    and a basic bus-mutex analysis.
